A Distinguished Trainer, Judge & Competitor

Marilyn is a distinguished trainer, competitor, judge, and breeder of horses for eventing and dressage. She was President of the Ground Jury at the 2016 Rio Olympics and a member of the Ground Jury at the 2008 Beijing Olympics. She was also President of the Ground Jury at the World Equestrian Games (WEG) in 2010 at the Kentucky Horse Park.

Marilyn works with all levels of riders at Applewood Farm, and is well known for bringing the average horse and rider to their full potential. Experienced eventing and dressage schoolmasters with winning track records are available for lessons at all levels. Applewood is a lively community where riders participate in clinics, schooling shows and lessons, plus sharing good times in the barn or out hacking.
