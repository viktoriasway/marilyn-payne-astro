Marilyn
Payne

Eventing  ~  Dressage  ~  Judging

Marilyn is a distinguished trainer, competitor, judge, and breeder of horses for eventing and dressage. She was President of the Ground Jury at the 2016 Rio Olympics and a member of the Ground Jury at the 2008 Beijing Olympics. She was also President of the Ground Jury at the World Equestrian Games (WEG) in 2010 at the Kentucky Horse Park.

Marilyn works with all levels of riders at Applewood Farm, and is well known for bringing the average horse and rider to their full potential. Experienced eventing and dressage schoolmasters with winning track records are available for lessons at all levels.

Applewood Farm

Training, Lessons, Clinics, Shows

The facilities are ideally suited for both Dressage and schooling Eventing, and include the following:

  • Indoor riding arena 70′ × 200′ with mirrors, heated viewing room and tack up area, sand and rubber footing
  • Outdoor ring 70′ × 200′ with sand and rubber footing
  • Schooling cross country course with water complex, ditches, banks, mounds, tables, logs, and a large variety of other obstacles
  • Large grass show jumping field
