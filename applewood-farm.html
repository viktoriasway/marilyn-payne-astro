Applewood Farm

A Place to learn, show and play!

The facilities are ideally suited for both Dressage and schooling Eventing, and include the following:

  • Indoor riding arena 70′ x 180′ with mirrors, heated viewing room and tack up area, sand and rubber footing
  • Outdoor ring 80′ x 200′ with sand and rubber footing
  • Schooling cross country course with water complex, ditches, banks, mounds, tables, logs, and a large variety of other obstacles
  • Large grass show jumping field

Applewood welcomes riders of all levels for Shows, Clinics, and Training.

Shows

2026 Shows

Applewood offers six ESDCTA-registered Dressage Shows, Combined Tests (dressage and show jumping) & Clear Round jumping for riders of all levels. There are also two USEA Young Event Horse (4- and 5-year olds) shows, and two Mini Event Competitions which include 4 levels (Starter, Beginner Novice, Novice and Training) for riders.

Clinics

2026 Clinics

Clinics are held throughout the year for both eventers and dressage riders. All horses and riders from entry level through advanced are welcome! Participants are scheduled in groups of four to six, at the appropriate level for their stage of development and their goals.

Training

Ride with Marilyn Payne, the Olympic judge whose specialty is bringing horse and rider to their full potential! Every rider receives personalized attention to their individual goals. Ride your own horse or one of Applewood’s experienced dressage and event horses to refine your skills.
